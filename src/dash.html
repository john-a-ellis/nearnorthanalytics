About the Labour Market Dash Board

North Bay Labour Market Dashboard is a tool to help you understand the labour market in North Bay, Ontario. It provides information on the labour force, employment, and unemployment in the region. The dashboard is updated regularly with the latest data from Statistics Canada. The data used in the dashboard comes from the Labour Force Survey (LFS) conducted by Statistics Canada. The LFS is a monthly survey that collects information on the labour market activities of Canadians. It provides data on the labour force, employment, and unemployment in Canada and its regions.
